About Max

Max Verstappen entered Formula 1 at just 17 and quickly proved he was special. His fearless driving made him the sport’s youngest race winner in 2016 and set him apart as a true wheel-to-wheel racer. After maturing into a dominant force, Verstappen claimed his first world title in 2021 and went on to become the defining driver of his generation, combining raw speed, consistency, and relentless ambition.
